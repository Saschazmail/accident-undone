This Privacy Policy describes how Accident Undone LLC ("Accident Undone," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when using our services, website at accidentundone.com, or when communicating with us by phone, text, or email. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Section 01

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you request our services, contact us by phone, text, or email, or submit information through our website.

Information You Provide

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Details about your accident — type, location, date, and circumstances
  • Physical location at the time of the accident (if provided)
  • Insurance information (if voluntarily shared)
  • Medical information relevant to your accident (if voluntarily shared)
  • Communications with our team via phone, text, or email

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited on our website and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • IP address and general geographic location
Section 02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve our accident services to you.

  • To connect you with transportation, medical providers, body shops, and attorneys appropriate to your situation
  • To communicate with you about your case and services we are coordinating on your behalf
  • To send appointment confirmations, service updates, and follow-up communications
  • To respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • To improve our website, services, and service partner network
  • To comply with legal obligations

We will never use your information to send unsolicited promotional messages unrelated to the services you have requested.

Section 03

Data Sharing

Customer data is not shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes.

Mobile opt-in and consent are never shared with anyone for any purpose. Any information sharing that may be mentioned elsewhere in this policy excludes mobile opt-in data and SMS consent entirely.

When We May Share Information

We may share limited information with our service partners — including transportation providers, medical providers, body shops, and attorneys — strictly and only to the extent necessary to provide the services you have requested.

We Never Share

  • Mobile opt-in data or SMS consent — under any circumstances, with anyone
  • Your information with third parties for marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes
  • Your information with data brokers or lead generation companies
  • Your personal information for any purpose other than fulfilling the services you requested

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Accident Undone, our clients, or the public.

Section 04

Accident Undone Messaging Terms and Conditions

The following terms apply to all SMS and MMS text message communications between Accident Undone LLC and individuals who have opted in to receive text messages from us.

SMS Terms & Conditions

  1. The messaging program consists of general conversational messaging to answer questions and provide support to customers.
  2. You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text STOP to the phone number from which you received messages. After you send the SMS message STOP to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us. If you want to join again, just sign up as you did the first time and we will start sending SMS messages to you again.
  3. If you are experiencing issues with the messaging program you can reply with the keyword HELP for more assistance, or you can get help directly at hello@accidentundone.com
  4. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
  5. As always, message and data rates may apply for any messages sent to you from us and to us from you. Message frequency will vary based on communication needs. If you have any questions about your text plan or data plan, it is best to contact your wireless provider.
  6. If you have any questions regarding privacy, please read our privacy policy contained in the rest of this document.

How to Opt In

You may opt in to receive SMS messages from Accident Undone by texting us directly, by providing your phone number through our website contact form and indicating consent, or by verbally consenting when speaking with an Accident Undone representative.

Types of Messages We Send

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Service status updates regarding your case
  • Responses to questions you have asked us
  • Follow-up communications related to services we are coordinating for you
  • Important information about your accident services

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your individual case and communication needs. We only send messages relevant to the services being provided to you.

Section 05

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

Section 06

Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Opt-out of SMS — text STOP at any time to unsubscribe from text messages
  • Do Not Sell — we do not sell personal information

To exercise any of these rights contact us at hello@accidentundone.com or call 1-855-REWIND-0.

California Residents — CCPA

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. We do not sell personal information. California residents may submit data requests by contacting hello@accidentundone.com.

Nevada Residents

Nevada residents may opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. Contact us at hello@accidentundone.com with any questions.
